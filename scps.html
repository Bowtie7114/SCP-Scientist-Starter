Your SCPs

Note: These descriptions are NOT the full documentations of the SCPs in question. Please follow the footnotes for the full reports, containment procedures, and additional information regarding these anomalies.
The italicised sections are excerpts taken directly from the full documentations.

SCP-914

Object Class: Safe

SCP-914, The Clockworks, is a large clockwork device weighing several tons and covering an area of eighteen square meters, consisting of screw drives, belts, pulleys, gears, springs and other clockwork.
The words Rough, Coarse, 1:1, Fine, and Very Fine are positioned at points around the knob. Below the knob is a large “key” that winds the “mainspring”.

When an object is placed in the Intake Booth, a door slides shut, and a small bell sounds. If the knob is turned to any position and the key wound up, SCP-914 will “refine” the object in the booth.
No energy is lost in the process, and the object appears to be in stasis until the Output Booth door is opened.

As a safe class anomaly, SCP-914 will be one of your easier assignments: monitoring. Tests are conducted constantly so all we need is someone to ensure nothing changes.
You will check it thoroughly every week for anything new, and only this.

SCP-999

Object Class: Safe

SCP-999 appears to be a large, amorphous, gelatinous mass of translucent orange slime, weighing about 54 kg (120 lbs) with a consistency similar to that of peanut butter.
Subjects temperament is best described as playful and dog-like: when approached, SCP-999 will often react with overwhelming elation, slithering over to the nearest person and leaping upon them,
“hugging” them with a pair of pseudopods while nuzzling the persons face with a third pseudopod, all the while emitting high-pitched gurgling and cooing noises.
Simply touching SCP-999s surface causes an immediate mild euphoria, which intensifies the longer one is exposed to SCP-999, and lasts long after separation from the creature.

 As with everyone else at the Site, your role will be to play with SCP-999 when it appears bored, and you will be part of its food rotation duties.
SCP-999 is the kindest being you will ever come across - be sure to show it the same kindness.

SCP-847

Object Class: Euclid

SCP-847 is a human female mannequin, 156 cm tall and 27 kg in mass, constructed of human hair and an unknown composite fibrous polymer that abrades and shatters similarly to porcelain.
SCP-847 is animate and moves with erratic, stiff motions while shuddering to maintain balance. It demonstrates different behaviors depending on the genotypical sex and identified gender of nearby humans.

It is strongly advised that you read the full file on this SCP. Tests will involve discovering a means to change SCP-847s demeanor, or perhaps calmly and painlessly deactivating it by relieving its mental state.
However, please note that if you are do not have an XY chromosome set, and identify as female, you will be in severe danger within 50 meteres of SCP-847. Maintain caution at all times.

SCP-106

Object Class: Keter

SCP-106 appears to be an elderly humanoid, with a general appearance of advanced decomposition.
This appearance may vary, but the “rotting” quality is observed in all forms.
106 causes a “corrosion” effect in all solid matter it touches, engaging a physical breakdown in materials several seconds after contact.
This is observed as rusting, rotting, and cracking of materials, and the creation of a black, mucus-like substance

SCP-106 is a severe threat and must be managed carefully. As a Keter-Class anomaly with the ability to end your life faster than you can blink, it is advised you read through the containment procedures carefully. Your main task regarding SCP-106 is conceiving of a method of terminating the entity with as little collateral damage to SCP-staff and infrastructure as possible.
